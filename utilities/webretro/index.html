Get ROM files to play at romsgames.net/roms on your home computer or phone and email them to yourself.

Alternatively, you can put the ROMS in your Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive and load them from whichever drive you picked directly. For this, there are three little icons, one for each of the drives, at the bottom of the ROM Select Menu on the page after the Core Select.
